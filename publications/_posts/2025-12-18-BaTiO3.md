---
title: "Machine-learning interatomic potential for BaTiO3: phase transitions, domain walls, and grain boundaries"
journal: J. Phys.: Condens. Matter 38 015402
authors:
  - Amit Sehrawat
  - Karsten Albe
  - Jochen Rohrer
abstract: A machine learning interatomic potential for BaTiO3 is presented based on the atomic cluster expansion formalism, enabling atomistic simulations of phase transitions, defect structures, and domain walls. Trained on a comprehensive dataset of density-functional theory calculations, the potential effectively captures the sequence of temperature-driven phase transitions from rhombohedral to orthorhombic, tetragonal, and cubic phases. In addition, the effect of pressure on these phase transitions is well described showing a decrease in the transition temperatures with increasing pressure as observed experimentally. The transferability of the potential is exemplified by accurately predicting 180∘ domain-wall structures and the energetics of symmetric tilt grain boundaries in the rhombohedral phase.
